## 📝 Project Overview
This project performs a comprehensive data analysis of **41,359 Zomato food delivery transactions** to identify operational bottlenecks and provide strategic recommendations for improving delivery efficiency.

The analysis covers various factors including traffic density, weather conditions, vehicle types, and geographic locations (Urban vs. Semi-Urban vs. Metropolitan).

## 🚀 Key Findings
- **Average Delivery Time:** 26.6 minutes across all regions.
- **The Traffic Bottleneck:** Traffic density is the #1 factor affecting delivery, causing a **48% spike** in delivery times during peak jams.
- **Geographic Gap:** There is a significant performance gap between Urban areas (23 min avg) and Semi-Urban areas (49 min avg).
- **Service Excellence:** Despite logistical challenges, delivery personnel maintain an exceptional average rating of **4.63/5**.

## 📈 Strategic Recommendations
1. **Traffic-Aware Routing:** Implement intelligent routing to bypass high-density traffic zones.
2. **Semi-Urban Hubs:** Establish micro-hubs in semi-urban areas to reduce the 49-minute delivery average.
3. **Vehicle Maintenance:** Optimize vehicle condition management to ensure consistent performance across the fleet.
